While Attributes are the baseline potential of a character, Abilities are instead what the character knows and has learned how to do. Being strong is one thing, but throwing a clumsy punch isn't going to win you a fight. This is why when rolls are made, Attributes are normally paired with a suitable Ability to match the action being taken.

There are 30 Abilities in total: 10 talents, skills, and Knowledges. Each Ability encompasses a broad range of uses, though others (Academics, Crafts, Expression, Law, Occult, Performance, Science, and Technology) might require a specialty even if it isn't at 4 or higher to illustrate what area they focus in.

<div class = "leftHeader" id="specialties">Specialties</div>

Not everyone is good at the same things in the same way. A skilled marksman might be proficient with rifles while another pistols. An academic might have studied European history while someone else focused on linguistics. To portray this, all characters with ratings of 4 or higher in either Attributes or Abilities can choose Specialties for those traits.

A specialty is a specific subcategory of the Attribute or Ability. Someone with a dexterity rating of 4 might be good at sprinting, while a character with Crafts 5 might be an impressive painter. Whenever a roll is made, and the action would fall under the chosen specialty, any die that rolls a 10 is counted as 2 successes rather than 1.

All of the specialties listed below are examples, others can be made up so long as they aren't too broad.

<div class="leftHeader" id="talents">Talents</div>
<em>Talents describe what you intuitively know, what you can do without coaching or instruction. The only way to improve your Talents is through direct experience — with the exception of a very few cases (such as studying a text on Jeet Kune Do to learn a single dot of Brawl), these things can’t be learned from a book or an Internet video. If you try an action involving a Talent your character doesn’t possess, there’s no penalty to your basic Attribute dice pool. Talents are so intuitive that virtually everyone can execute them with some degree of capacity.</em>

<div class="secondHeader" id="alertness">Alertness</div>
This is your basic knack for noticing things that go on around you, even when you’re not actively looking for them. Alertness describes the attention you pay to the outside world, whether otherwise occupied or not. This Talent is typically paired with Perception, and is best used when sensing physical stimuli (as opposed to moods or clues).

<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Novice:</b> You’re no mindless drone. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Practiced:</b> Habitual eavesdropper. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Competent:</b> You keep a sharp eye on your surroundings. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Expert:</b> Whether from paranoia or good sense, you are rarely caught off-guard. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Master:</b> Your senses are on par with those of a wild animal.

<b>Posessed by:</b> Hunters, Bodyguards, Security Personnel, Journalists, Burglars <br>
<b>Specialties:</b> Noises, Eavesdropping, Fine Details, Hidden Weapons, Crowds, Forests, Animals

<div class="secondHeader" id="athletics">Athletics</div>
This Talent represents your basic athletic ability, as well as any training you might have had in sports or other rigorous activities. Athletics concerns all forms of running, jumping, throwing, swimming, sports, and the like. However, Athletics doesn’t cover basic motor actions such as lifting weights, nor does it govern athletic feats covered by another Ability (such as Melee).

<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Novice:</b> You had an active childhood. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Practiced:</b> High-school athlete. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Competent:</b> Talented lifelong amateur. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Expert:</b> Professional athlete. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Master:</b> Olympic medalist.

<b>Posessed by:</b> Athletes, Hobbyists, Park Rangers, Jocks, Kids <br>
<b>Specialties:</b> Swimming, Rock Climbing, Acrobatics, Dancing, Parkour, specific sports

<div class="secondHeader" id="awareness">Awareness</div>
Awareness is an instinctual reaction to the presence of the supernatural. It differs from Alertness (which measures sensitivity to mundane events) and Occult which covers actual knowledge about the supernatural). Usually, only supernatural creatures have access to this particular Talent, but some unique mortals have a sense that something is strange in the world (such as those with True Faith – see p. 372).

Characters with Awareness sometimes get hunches, chills, or sudden flashes of inspiration when they are near supernatural creatures, objects, or events. This insight is purely subconscious, and knowing that something’s wrong doesn’t mean that the character knows what it is. To get more specific information, the Kindred will need to use Occult or a particular power – Awareness only makes the vampire more receptive to the presence of the unusual.

A vampire can use Awareness deliberately if he suspects that something is supernatural, but more often the Storyteller can ask for an Awareness roll to determine whether a vampire notices a strange event that’s not immediately visible.

<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Novice:</b> Once in a while, you get the feeling that something isn’t right. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Practiced:</b> You sometimes get strange vibes from a particular direction or vague area (like a building). <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Competent:</b> You can walk into a room and know that something unusual is going on within. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Expert:</b> If you concentrate, you can sense whether a someone in a group of people or a collection of objects is supernatural. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Master:</b> You instinctually know if something or someone is mundane or supernatural.

<b>Posessed by:</b> Psychics, New Agers, Mystics, Paranormal Researchers <br>
<b>Specialties:</b> Ghostly Activity, Mystical Objects, Someone’s In My Head, Debunking

<div class="secondHeader" id="brawl">Brawl</div>
The Brawl Talent represents how well you fight in tooth-and-nail situations. This Talent represents skill in unarmed combat, whether from formal martial arts training or simply from plenty of experience. Effective brawlers are coordinated, resistant to pain, quick, strong, and mean. The willingness to do whatever it takes to hurt your opponent wins plenty of fights.

<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Novice:</b> You were picked on as a kid. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Practiced:</b> You’ve participated in the occasional barroom tussle. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Competent:</b> You’ve fought regularly and routinely, and generally walked away in better shape than your opponents. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Expert:</b> You could be a serious contender on the MMA circuit. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Master:</b> Somewhere on the Internet, there’s a video of you taking down three men in four seconds.

<b>Posessed by:</b> Military, Police, Roughnecks, Thugs <br>
<b>Specialties:</b> Dirty Fighting, Strikes, Throws, Submission Holds, specific martial arts or combat styles

<div class="secondHeader" id="empathy">Empathy</div>
You understand the emotions of others, and can sympathize with, feign sympathy for, or play on such emotions as you see fit. You are adept at discerning motive, and might be able to discern when someone’s lying to you. However, you may be so in tune with other people’s feelings that your own emotions are affected.

<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Novice:</b> You lend the occasional shoulder to cry on. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Practiced:</b> You can sometimes literally feel someone else’s suffering. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Competent:</b> You have a keen insight into other people’s motivations. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Expert:</b> It’s almost impossible to lie to you. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Master:</b> The human soul conceals no mysteries from you.

<b>Posessed by:</b> Social Workers, Parents, Actors, Psychologists, Detectives, Seducers, Mediums, Best Friends <br>
<b>Specialties:</b> Emotions, Insight, Motives, Gaining Trust

<div class="secondHeader" id="expression">Expression</div>
This is your ability to get your point across clearly, whether through conversation, poetry, or even in 140 characters or fewer. Characters with high Expression can phrase their opinions or beliefs in a manner that cannot be ignored (even if their opinions are misinformed or worthless). They might also be talented actors, skilled at conveying moods or communicating emotion with every gesture. Additionally, this Talent represents your ability for poetry, creative writing, or other literary art forms. For many elders, Expression is the subtle art of crafting a satirical epigram capable of socially crippling one’s longtime rival. For younger Kindred, Expression may well be the key to convincing thirty stake-wielding Anarchs to converge on the Sheriff’s private hunting ground with the right text message. You can choose a specialty in Expression, even at less than 4 dots.

<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Novice:</b> Your talent has matured past crude poetry on notebook paper. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Practiced:</b> You could lead a college debate team. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Competent:</b> You could be a successful writer. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Expert:</b> Your work is Pulitzer material. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Master:</b> Steve Jobs asks you for input on his next mobile device.

<b>Posessed by:</b> Actors, Writers, Poets, Politicians, Journalists, Web Personalities, Rabble-Rousers <br>
<b>Specialties:</b> Acting, Poetry, Fiction, Impromptu, Conversation, Social Media

<div class="secondHeader" id="intimidation">Intimidation</div>
Intimidation takes many forms, from outright threats and physical violence to mere force of personality. It needn’t be course or callous, and a well-placed intimidating word under the right circumstances might well be called “diplomacy” in certain circles. You know the right method for each occasion, and can be very… persuasive.

<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Novice:</b> Shady teenager. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Practiced:</b> Skinhead thug. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Competent:</b> Drill sergeant. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Expert:</b> Your air of authority cows casual passersby. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Master:</b> You can frighten off vicious animals.

<b>Posessed by:</b> Bullies, Anonymous, Military Officers, Bouncers, Gangsters, Sabbat <br>
<b>Specialties:</b> Veiled Threats, Pulling Rank, Physical Coercion, Blackmail, Internet

<div class="secondHeader" id="leadership">Leadership</div>
You are an example to others and can inspire them to do what you want. Leadership has less to do with manipulating people’s desires than it does with presenting yourself as the sort of person they want to follow. Anyone can lead a group into some sort of conflict; a good leader can get them back out intact. This Talent is usually paired with Charisma rather than Manipulation.

<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Novice:</b> Captain of your Little League team. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Practiced:</b> Student body president. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Competent:</b> An effective CEO. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Expert:</b> Presidential material. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Master:</b> You could be beloved dictator of a nation.

<b>Posessed by:</b> Politicians, Princes, Managers, Executives, Military Officers, Police <br>
<b>Specialties:</b> Oratory, Compelling, Friendly, Open, Noble, Military, Multimedia

<div class="secondHeader" id="streetwise">Streetwise</div>
The streets can provide a lot of information or money to those who know the language. Streetwise allows you to blend in unobtrusively with the local scene, pick up gossip, understand slang, or even dabble in criminal doings.

<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Novice:</b> You know who’s holding. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Practiced:</b> You’re accorded respect on the street. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Competent:</b> You could head your own gang. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Expert:</b> You have little to fear in even the worst neighborhoods. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Master:</b> If you haven’t heard it, it hasn’t been said.

<b>Posessed by:</b> Criminals, Homeless People, Reporters, Clubgoers, Savvy Detectives, Neonates <br>
<b>Specialties:</b> Fencing, Illegal Drugs, Illegal Weapons, Free Wifi, Gangs, Being On the Guest List, Local Slang

<div class="secondHeader" id="subterfuge">Subterfuge</div>
You know how to conceal your own motives and project what you wish. Furthermore, if you can root out other people’s motives, you can then use those motives against them. This Talent defines your talent for intrigue, secrets, and double-dealing. Mastery of Subterfuge can make you the ultimate seducer or a brilliant spy.

<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Novice:</b> You tell the occasional white lie. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Practiced:</b> Vampire. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Competent:</b> Criminal lawyer. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Expert:</b> Deep-cover agent. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Master:</b> You’re the very last person anyone would suspect.

<b>Posessed by:</b> Politicians, Lawyers, Vampires, Teenagers, Con Men, Pick-up Artists <br>
<b>Specialties:</b> Seduction, Impeccable Lies, Feigning Mortality, the Long Con

<div class="leftHeader" id="skills">Skills</div>
<em>Skills are Abilities learned through training, apprenticeships, or other instruction. If you try to perform an action involving a Skill in which you have no rating, your difficulty is increased by one. An unskilled worker just isn’t as effective as someone who might have lower Attributes but a better understanding of what the procedure entails.</em>

<div class="secondHeader" id="animal-ken">Animal Ken</div>
You can understand animals’ behavior patterns. This Skill allows you to predict how an animal might react in a given situation, train a domesticated creature, or even try to calm or enrage animals.

<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Novice:</b> You can get a domesticated horse to let you pet it. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Practiced:</b> You can housebreak a puppy. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Competent:</b> You could train a seeing-eye dog. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Expert:</b> Circus trainer. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Master:</b> You can tame wild beasts without benefit of supernatural powers.

<b>Posessed by:</b> Farmers, Animal Trainers, Zookeepers, Veterinarians, Pet Owners, Domitors <br>
<b>Specialties:</b> Dogs, Attack Training, Big Cats, Horses, Farm Animals, Falconry

<div class="secondHeader" id="crafts">Crafts</div>
This Skill covers your ability to make or fix things with your hands. Crafts allows you to work in fields such as carpentry, leatherworking, weaving, or even mechanical expertise such as car repair. You can even create lasting works of art with this Skill, depending on the number of successes you achieve. You must always choose a specialization in Crafts, even though you retain some skill in multiple fields.

<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Novice:</b> High school wood shop. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Practiced:</b> You’re starting to develop your own style. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Competent:</b> You could start your own shop. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Expert:</b> You wrote instruction manuals on your field of specialization. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Master:</b> Your craftsmanship and insight is virtually without peer.

<b>Posessed by:</b> Mechanics, Artisans, Artists, Designers, Inventors, Back-to-the-Land Types <br>
<b>Specialties:</b> Pottery, Sewing, Home Repair, Carpentry, Appraisal, Carburetors

<div class="secondHeader" id="drive">Drive</div>
You can drive a car, and maybe other vehicles as well. This Skill does not automatically entail familiarity with complicated vehicles such as tanks or 18-wheelers, and difficulties may vary depending on your experience with individual automobiles. After all, helming a station wagon doesn’t prepare you for double-clutching a Maserati at 100 miles per hour.

<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Novice:</b> You know how to work as automatic transmission. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Practiced:</b> You can drive a stick shift. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Competent:</b> Professional trucker. <br>

You have at least a basic understanding of most of the physical sciences, such as chemistry, biology, physics, and geology. This Knowledge can be put to all forms of practical use. In most cases, a player should select a specialty to reflect a focus for her character’s scientific studies, but this isn’t strictly necessary.

<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Student:</b> You know most of the high-school basics. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>College:</b> You’re familiar with the major theories. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Masters:</b> You could teach high-school science. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Doctorate:</b> You’re fully capable of advancing the knowledge in your field. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Scholar:</b> Your Nobel Prize is waiting for you.

<b>Posessed by:</b> Scientists, Students, Researchers, Teachers, Engineers, Technicians, Pilots <br>
<b>Specialties:</b> Chemistry, Biology, Geology, Physics, Astronomy

<div class="secondHeader" id="technology">Technology</div>
The Technology Knowledge represents a broad acumen with electronics, computer hardware, and devices more elaborate than “machines,” which fall under the Crafts Skill. If it has a processor, a transistor, or an integrated circuit — if it’s electronic rather than electrical — manipulating it uses the Technology Knowledge. This is the wide-ranging Ability used to build one’s own computer, install (or subvert) a security system, repair a mobile phone, or kitbash a shortwave radio. You must always choose a specialization in Technology, even though you possess some skill in multiple fields.

<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Student:</b> You can perform simple modifications or repairs. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>College:</b> You could make your living in assembly or repair. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Masters:</b> You can design new technologies from a set of objective requirements. <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Doctorate:</b> For you, it’s not, “Can this be done?” but “How can this be done?” <br>
<i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i><i class="fas fa-circle fa-sm"></i> <b>Scholar:</b> A visionary in the field; you shape how people interact with their world through devices.

<b>Posessed by:</b> Engineers, Scientists, Defense Contractors, the Cable Guy <br>
<b>Specialties:</b> Telecom, Computers, Security, Communications, Improvised Solutions, Industrial Espionage
